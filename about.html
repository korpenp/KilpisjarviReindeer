Our Story

Founded in 2018 by brothers Lars-Aslak and Per-Henrik Tornensis, Kilpisjärvi Reindeer is rooted in one of the oldest reindeer herding families in Arctic Lapland. Mentored by our father Juha Tornensis and grandfather Piera Tornensis, the family harmonizes centuries-old Sámi traditions with sustainable tourism.

Recognized with the Good Travel Seal and Visit Finland Sustainability certification, we invite you to connect with the Arctic's essence through immersive cultural and nature-based experiences.

Our mission is to preserve and share the authentic Sámi way of life while protecting the fragile Arctic ecosystem that has sustained our family for generations.
