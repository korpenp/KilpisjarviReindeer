Experience Packages

PACKAGE 1

Sámi Experience

This program offers you a comprehensive information about the Sami culture and way of life

Includes:

  • "Year of the Reindeer" Audiovisual Story: A seasonal journey of reindeer herding in the Käsivarsi Wilderness Reserve, narrated by Per-Henrik.
  • Close Interaction: Questions and answer session
  • Reindeer Meet: Meet our friendly reindeer in a safe corral. Feed them lichen and capture unforgettable moments.
  • Cozy Cabin Relaxation: Warm up by an open fire in our log cabin with homemade biscuits and berry juice while learning about Sámi culture.
  • Optional: Serve traditional smoked reindeer meat (Additional cost)

How the Experience goes

Firstly the customers are directed to the Kammi (Cabin) introduction and program information is given by either Lars Aslak or Per-henrik Tornensis.

"Year of the Reindeer" Audiovisual Story followed by berry juice, homemade cookie. Traditional smoked reindeer meat if ordered.

After the video Lars tells more about the reindeer, life of the Sámi people and customers can ask questions.

After the question and answer section safety instructions are given and customers go out to meet the reindeer, feed and take pictures.

Optional Add-on

Reindeer sleigh ride can be added to the program for additional fee (length of ride 700m)

€80 per person

Group price: €50 (minimum 10 people)

Duration: 2 hours
